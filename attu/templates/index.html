Passages

This site offers a unique way to prepare scriptural content in your lessons. It is not a very browsable site.

The best approach is to just type the scriptures you want to show into the url bar. Here are a few examples.

  • http://{{ config.BASE_ADDRESS }}/1 Nephi 3:7
  • http://{{ config.BASE_ADDRESS }}/Moroni 10:3-5
  • http://{{ config.BASE_ADDRESS }}/3 Nephi 2:1-4; 4:1-2

You can try an alternate style.

  • http://{{ config.BASE_ADDRESS }}/tech/Moroni 10:3-5
